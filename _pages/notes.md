Welcome to Done Taiwan Series. This is a series of two books that aims to help students prepare for the entrance examinations for top mathematics master's programs in Taiwan. Each book includes solutions and corrections to the exams administered by National Taiwan University over the past 10 years.

Just like all mathematical texts, my book may contain some minor errors. If you believe you see an error or are confused by my writing, feel free to contact me.
